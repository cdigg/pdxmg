It Came From ... The Skies!

Location: Rae Loft, Portland, OR

Date: September 28, 2024

Fifty players. Six hours. Join us for a geopolitical near-future extraterrestrial first-contact megagame. Part board game, part role-playing game, part improv. Step into the role of a nation's Head of State, Foreign Minister, Chief Scientist, or Minister of Defense, or join the Extraterrestrial faction to achieve your goals and craft an unforgettable narrative of global emergencies and strategic gameplay.
