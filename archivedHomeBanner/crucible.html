Crucible of Nations, Nov 15 2025 @ 10 AM

Location: Rae Loft, Portland, OR

Date: Nov 15, 2025

Crucible of Nations is a fast-paced geopolitical megagame, where teams of players guide their fictional nation through a period of great upheaval. You will face challenges such as diplomacy, trade, war, espionage, and internal stability, while striving to achieve your national objectives and holding your unique civilization together. You will also interact with other nations through a dynamic game system, where you can negotiate, bluff, threaten, or cooperate.

Will you forge lasting alliances, or betray your friends at the right moment? Will you lead your nation to glory, or witness its downfall? Whether you're a seasoned megagamer or a newcomer to the genre, Crucible of Nations has something for everyone.

In this fantasy world international superpower has recently suffered a devastating civil war, creating a power vacuum unseen in centuries. Meanwhile, 7 other countries are reaching pivotal national crossroads, forced to make decisions that will define their civilizations for generations.

For Portland Megagames' run of this title, we will be in the beautiful Rae Loft building in NE Portland. The game runs from 10am-5pm and there will be a 45 minute lunch break. There is plenty of food places nearby but you are welcome to bring your own lunch! Email details will be sent out, including a casting questionnaire asking about your preferences for teams and roles, as well as a team and role briefings and rules closer to the game itself. We are excited to host this and hope you'll join us!

We will be teaching the game at the beginning of the day and you won't need to bring anything.

Costumes are encouraged but are by no means necessary.

There will be an after-game debrief/hangout at Culmination Brewing for those who are interested.
